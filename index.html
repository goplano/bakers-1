Instructions
  1. mix flour and water - rest 30 mins
  2. mix in starter - rest 30 minutes
  3. mix in salt - rest 30 minutes
  4. stretch and fold 1 - rest 30 minutes
  5. stretch and fold 2 - rest 30 minutes
  6. stretch and fold 3
  7. bulk fermentation - 3 - 3.5 hours
  8. pre-shape - reset 20 minutes
  9. final shape + secure bottom seam
  10. proof for 30 minutes
  11. retard in fridge - 16 hours
  12. pre-heat oven to 500
  13. bake lid on 25 minutes
  14. remove lid bake for 25 more minutes @450
  15. let cool for at least 3 hours
